The Algorithmist::Circle-Line Segment Intersection Demo

The demo illustrates the computation of intersection points between a line segment and a circle. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the line segment endpoints.

NOTICE the circle elongation due to graph aspect ratio, i.e. px per unit x != px per unit y.
