The Algorithmist::Star Shape Demo

This demo illustrates the creation of Star shapes from three to eight points that are defined by a bounding rectangle and an adjustable thickness parameter in (0.1, 0.9) that adjusts the Star from nearly linear spikes to nearly an n-gon in shape. After dragging a bounding box, polygon vertex coordinates are queried and the Star is drawn using EaselJS.

 
Points     Thickness  

Select the number of Star points and thickness, then click and drag a rectangle in the drawing area to create the shape.
