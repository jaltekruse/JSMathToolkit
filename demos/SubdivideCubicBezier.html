The Algorithmist::Subdivide Cubic Bezier Demo

The demo illustrates the subdivision of a cubic Bezier curve in 2D space into two equivalent cubic Beziers. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the four control points. Click on the numerical stepper to subdivide the cubic curve at the specified natural (t) parameter. The 'left' curve is drawn in blue an the 'right' curve in red.

 
Subdivision Parameter  

Drag the circular Markers to change control points. Click the numerical stepper to subdivide the curve at that parameter value.
