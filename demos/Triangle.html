The Algorithmist::Triangle Shape Demo

The triangle demo illustrates the creation of equilateral, isosceles, and arbitrary triangles using a bounding rectangle and two additional parameters (that apply only to arbitrary triangles). Right triangles are also possible to create, but are not shown in this particular demo (they use a secondary parameter to indicate the orientation of the righ-triangle along the minor dimension). After dragging a bounding box, polygon vertex coordinates are queried and the triangle is drawn using EaselJS.
