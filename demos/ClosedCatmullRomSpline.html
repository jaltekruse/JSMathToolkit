The Algorithmist::Closed Catmull-Rom Spline Demo

This demo is shows how to create a closed-loop Catmull-Rom spline. The point sequence should form a generally closed shape for the algorihtm to provide the most visually pleasing result. Quadratic Bezier approximation of the spline is rendered in blue.
