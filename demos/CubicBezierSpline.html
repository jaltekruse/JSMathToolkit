The Algorithmist::Cubic Bezier Spline Demo

The demo illustrates the parametric equation of a cubic Bezier curve in 2D space. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the interpolation points. Drag the vertical GraphSlice tool to query spline data. The spline (after quadratic approximation) is drawn with EaselJS, and drawing is completely decoupled from the computational module.
