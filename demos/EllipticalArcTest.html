The Algorithmist::Elliptical Arc Demo

The demo exercies the Elliptical Arc complex shape class. All complex shapes work by computing the shape given a number of parameters and then returning a draw stack. The draw stack consists of simple SVG-style commands that can be used to draw the shape onto a Canvas or convert them to an SVG path. Adjust the numerical stepper to set the ellipse rotation in degrees, then click the 'Start' button to draw the complete ellipse.

 
Ellipse Rotation  

The entire ellipse is drawn in successive steps of increasing arc from 0 to 2PI radians.
