The Algorithmist::Polynomial Least Squares

This demo illustrates a polynomial (quad, cubic, quartic) least squares fit to a set of sample data. Quadratic is drawn in white, cubic in blue, and quartic in yellow. Manually adjust the coordinates in the JS file to see the effect on the fit.

Check console for fit statistics of the various polynomials.
