The Algorithmist::Wind Motion Controller

The demo illustrates an object moving under the Wnind motion controller. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the wind vector endpoints (wind moves green to red), set the speed, then click 'Begin'. The launch angle is 45-degrees (upward and to the right) at 5 units/second and the drag is controlled by a single constant (and is presumed to be directly proportional to velocity)

 
Wind Speed    

Motion is over a ten-second interval with 0.5 graph units = 1 meter
