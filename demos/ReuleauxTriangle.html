The Algorithmist::Modified Reuleaux Triangle Shape Demo

This demo illustrates the creation of a modified Reuleaux Trianglete that is defined by a bounding rectangle and an adjustable 'expand' parameter in (0,1]. After dragging a bounding box, adjust the expand parameter.

 
Expand Factor  

Unlike a Reuleaux triangle, sides of the modified version are constructed from quadratic Beziers that move further away from linear as the 'expand' parameter increases.
