The Algorithmist::Graph Slice Tool Demo

The Graph slice tool overlays a 2D graph and is used to identify or select lines of constant-x or constant-y value. The tool may be dragged horizontally or vertically and new x- or y-values are sent to a callback function. The graph slice tool is written on top of Easel JS.

Drag the triangular marker to slice the graph and observe the new coordinate values.
