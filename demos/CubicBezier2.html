The Algorithmist::Cubic Bezier Arc Length Demo

The demo illustrates the relationship between natural parameter and arc length of a cubic Bezier. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the four control points. Adjust the numerical stepper to set a fraction of arc length along the curve and observe the difference between that fraction and the natural (t) parameter.

 
Normalized Arc Length  

Drag the slice tool (triangular markers) to query the natural curve parameter - there may be three at a single x-value - and other data. Drag the circular Markers to change control points. Cubic plotted in white and quad. approximation in blue.
