The Algorithmist::Curved C-Section Demo

The demo exercises the Circular C-Section complex shape class. All complex shapes work by computing the shape given a number of parameters and then returning a draw stack. The draw stack consists of simple SVG-style commands that can be used to draw the shape onto a Canvas or convert them to an SVG path. Click the 'Start' button to draw a C-Section over an interval of arc.

 

The circular C-Section is drawn in successive steps of increasing arc from 0 to 4PI/3 radians.
