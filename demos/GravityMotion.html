The Algorithmist::Gravity Motion Controller

The demo illustrates an object moving under the gravity motion controller. This controller has constant velocity along a vector and acceleration due to gravity. Drag the circular Markers to adjust the vector endpoints, set the speed, then click 'Begin'.

 
Speed    

Motion is over an eight-second interval with 0.5 graph units = 1 meter
